Who We Are

A Haven for Knowledge Seekers

The Institute of Liberal Studies (ILS) is an organisation devoted to learning. With over 50 years of continuous service, ILS has shaped the thoughts and attitudes of more than a thousand people across Coimbatore.

Every week, ILS presents a two-part programme. First — our signature Minute a Member — gives every member one minute to share anything under the sun. The second part brings guest lectures, debates, quizzes, and discussions.

ILS is open to all who have the desire to listen, learn, and lead. Membership is free, for life, for all ages.

What We Do

Our Programmes

🎤
Guest Lecture
Invited talks from distinguished guests sharing insights and experiences with our members.
📢
Special Lecture
Talks from our members on topics of broad intellectual, social, or cultural significance.
💬
Group Discussion
Moderated exchanges where members explore ideas, challenge assumptions, and share perspectives.
🔭
Kaleidoscope
A multi-member, multi-perspective talk where diversity of thoughts are gained on a topic of importance.
🧠
Quiz
Knowledge meets adrenaline as our members contest their wisdom and stretch understanding.
⚖️
Debate
Structured argumentation that sharpens critical thinking and fosters civil discourse.
Annual Memorial Lecture
Narayanasamy Memorial Lecture
In memory of Late Mr. Narayanasamy, our annual lecture on the first week of January is dedicated to unravelling Tamil literature.
Annual Memorial Lecture
Veeraraghavan Memorial Lecture
An annual tribute commemorating Late Mr. Veeraraghavan's legacy on the first week of October, exploring the nuances of the English language.
