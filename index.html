Who We Are

A Haven for Those Who Seek Knowledge

The Institute of Liberal Studies (ILS) is an organisation devoted to learning. With over 50 years of continuous service, ILS has shaped the thoughts and attitudes of more than a thousand people across Coimbatore.

Every week, ILS presents a two-part programme. The first — our signature Minute a Member — gives every member one minute to share anything under the sun. The second part brings in guest lectures, debates, quizzes, group discussions, and more.

ILS is open to all who have the desire to listen, learn, and lead. Membership is free, for life, for all ages and genders.
